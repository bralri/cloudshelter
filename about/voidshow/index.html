Corpse
2022
Series of looped digital videos

The Corpse are looped digital videos each assembled from fragments of existing film and animation. Their construction follows the Surrealist game Exquisite Corpse in which head, torso, and legs are combined to create new creatures. The work stems from research into the undead nature of images in the digital world.
